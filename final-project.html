Final Project Proposal

Over the past few months, I’ve admittedly been on a ramen noodle binge… Tearing through seasoning packets like it’s my divine calling, I cannot resist the allure of the noodle. But, unfortunately, this addiction can’t follow me to my dorm room––I don’t have a microwave. Instead of taking the easy way out and buying one, I instead want to invent a single serving ramen maker.

In my head, I’m imagining something that looks like a mini rice cooker. You would put the dry noodles into the device with water, turn the heater on, and, 5 to 10 minutes later, you’re in business. Like a rice cooker, there would be a metal bowl that’s placed into the cooker, where the actual water and noodles go. The bowl is removable for washing. The bowl could also possibly be eaten out of, if I also invent a type of holder for it so it doesn’t burn the user.
